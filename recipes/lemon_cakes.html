Sansa's Lemon Cakes

Shall we have some Lemon Cakes?

Sansa’s favorite dessert, this expensive treats are enjoyed by upper-class noblewoman of Seven Kindows, in Game of Thrones, where they are a mainstay at the refined social gatherings of noble courts

Ingredients

  • 2 sicilian lemons
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 tablespoons lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • ½ teaspoon of baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon of bicarbonate of soda
  • 200g butter at room temperature
  • 1 cup of refined sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • ¾ cup lemon juice
  • 1 ½ cups refined sugar

Steps

  1. Cut the lemons into very thin slices and boil in 1 cup of water with 1 cup of sugar for 15 minutes over medium heat
  2. Mix the lemon zest and juice with the milk and let it rest while you make the rest of the recipe
  3. In a bowl, mix the flour, salt, baking powder and bicarbonate
  4. In another, beat the butter with the sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy
  5. Add the eggs, one at a time, beating after each addition
  6. Add the dry ingredients alternating with the milk, mixing well between each step
  7. Grease a muffin tin and place a lemon slice at the bottom of each space
  8. Fill ⅔ of the height of each one with the dough
  9. Bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for about 20 minutes
  10. Remove from oven and let cool
  11. Make small holes with a toothpick in each cupcake and let it soak in the lemon syrup
  12. Wait 5 to 10 minutes before serving, lemon side up
  13. To prepare the syrup: Mix the lemon juice with the cups refined sugar and heat until the sugar dissolves
